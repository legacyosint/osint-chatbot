Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Legacy OSINT ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our AI-powered OSINT assistant application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, including threat analysis and image recognition, we collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Email Address (collected when you create an account).
  • User Content: Images and text queries you voluntarily upload for analysis.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store credit card details on our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic log data (IP address, device type) for security and debugging.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for:

  • Performing image recognition and website scraping as requested by you.
  • Managing your login session and Premium subscription status.
  • Enforcing daily usage limits (e.g., 2 uploads/day for free users).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted third-party service providers necessary to run the app:

  • Stripe: Payment processing.
  • Render: Cloud hosting.

5. App Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions on your Android device:

  • Storage / Photos: To allow you to select images for upload.
  • Internet Access: To connect to our AI servers.

6. Data Retention

Uploaded images are processed transiently for analysis. We do not use your private images to train public AI models without your explicit consent. Account data is retained only as long as your account is active.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our Support Page.
